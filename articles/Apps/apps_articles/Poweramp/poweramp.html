Poweramp - The (real) ultimate music app

Poweramp is a very powerful, higly customizable and feature-rich music player for android.

Some screens you can get inside of poweramp

Here are (in my opinion) the key highlights of this wonderful app :

  • Very smooth and fluid interface
  • Handles playlists (.m3u files) very well when associated with audio files on SD cards (which often breaks after reboots on most apps) as well as on internal storage
  • Amazing equalizer with good presets (it even feels like the audio quality gets upgraded)
  • Wave-form progress bar (very convenient to localize drops)
  • Adding artwork to the files missing one
  • Very wide range of deep settings
  • Many different layouts and themes
